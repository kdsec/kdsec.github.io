Tingwen Liu /

Tingwen Liu is an Associate Researcher of the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Associate Professor of the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received his Ph.D. degree from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2013, under the supervision of Binxing Fang and Li Guo. My research interests include Information Extraction (IE) and Knowledge Fusion, Text Understanding and Semantic Computing, Heterogeneous Network Representation, etc. [切换到中文页面]
